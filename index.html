03 / About ● A Red Oak family trade

Fifty years
one standard.

“We've been doing this since nineteen seventy-four — the work hasn't changed, just the lights we put it under.”
— Dan Dougherty, President

O'Neal Electric Company was founded in 1974 by John O'Neal in Red Oak, Iowa. Across thirty-seven years, John built an electrically versatile company engaging every corner of the trade across southwest Iowa — industrial floors, Main Street storefronts, farmhouses, and county jobs.

In 2009, John retired and handed the keys to current president Dan Dougherty. The shop hasn't changed: the same values, the same crew-first attitude, the same promise to show up and do it right. Nearly fifty years in, we're still doing what we do.
